• User-initiated: Reads page text only after you click "Generate Quiz".
  • Privacy-first: No data sent to servers, no analytics.

    • How it works

    1. Open a Grokipedia article in Chrome.
    2. Click the extension icon or the floating "Quiz Me" button to open the quiz popup.
    3. Choose difficulty and the number of questions, then click Generate Quiz.
    4. The extension extracts the visible article text and uses Chrome's on-device AI to create multiple-choice questions locally.
    5. Review, answer, and get immediate feedback — everything stays on your device.

    Permissions required

    We ask for the smallest set of permissions needed to provide the extension’s functionality. You will be prompted before any page content is accessed.

    • activeTab — Temporarily reads the active Grokipedia tab only after you click Generate.
    • scripting — Safely extracts the page’s visible text to generate quiz questions.
    • contextMenus — Optional: adds a right-click menu item for quick access.

    FAQ

    Q: Does the extension send my content to external servers?

    A: No. All processing uses Chrome’s on-device AI model. Nothing leaves your browser.

    Q: Is this an official Grokipedia product?

    A: No. This is an independent third-party extension and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Grokipedia.
